The problem

Recommendation algorithms narrow you the longer you use them.

The big music apps optimize for “you’ll definitely like this.” That’s safe, profitable — and a filter bubble. After six months on Spotify, your weekly mix is the same 80 artists in different order. Claudio is built to deliberately push the other way.

Algorithmic radio

How it works

Four steps, every pick.

01

Read context

Time, weather, your last 30 days of plays, what you just said, the loves and skips.

02

Pick something new

An LLM is constrained to choose an artist outside your recent rotation, dialed by your novelty knob.

03

Explain it

One sentence that ties the pick back to a specific context signal you’d recognize.

04

You react

Heart, skip, or talk back. All three feed the next pick. The loop runs every track.
